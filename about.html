About Us

Personal care for your healthy living

At Mamatha Hospital, we are committed to your health and well-being. Our comprehensive services include general medicine, maternity and women's health, emergency and critical care, orthopedics, pediatrics, ENT, cardiology, neurology, urology, nephrology, gastroenterology, dermatology, psychiatry, and oncology.
We offer advanced diagnostic and imaging services, along with support from our on-site pharmacy and optical services. Our 24/7 emergency care and fully equipped ICU ensure that we are here for you whenever you need us.
